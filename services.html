Services

At AIRCORE, we don’t just manufacture hollow bearing balls—we engineer advanced, customized components to meet your most challenging design requirements. Our team can integrate precision features directly into our hollow forms, including holes, internal or external threads, and press-fit or bonded shafts. These customizations allow our hollow balls to function as structural components, fasteners, or interfaces within complex assemblies.

Our proprietary forming techniques also allow us to control wall thickness within a single ball, enabling material to be distributed exactly where it’s needed—for balance, strength, or weight optimization. Whether you're compensating for off-axis loading, reducing inertia, or fine-tuning mass distribution, we provide precise solutions with performance in mind.

In addition to spherical geometries, AIRCORE manufactures hollow spheroids and a variety of custom geometric solids with trapped or occluded internal volumes. These shapes can serve roles beyond traditional bearings, including energy absorption, mass balancing, or functional enclosures in aerospace, medical, or electromechanical systems.

If your design pushes boundaries, we’re here to make sure your components can keep up. Let us help you integrate lighter, smarter, and more capable parts into your next innovation.
