Precision under punishment.

With exceptional heat resistance and wear performance, tungsten carbide thrives in punishing thermal and mechanical environments. Aircore Bearings tungsten carbide hollow bearing balls reduce component weight without sacrificing the durability critical to long-life systems.

Thermal stress?
No sweat.

Tungsten's exceptional density and melting point make it the material of choice for extreme environments.

Tungsten carbides resist thermal distortion, abrasion, and chemical attack. Hollow construction reduces surface contact forces, helping to cut down on system wear while maintaining the required strength.

Even in high-radiation, corrosive, or high-G environments, tungsten carbide balls hold their shape and deliver consistent performance.

Hollow tungsten carbide balls open new possibilities for faster motion cycles and less mechanical drag without giving up core performance.
